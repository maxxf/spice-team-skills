# Slack Summary Template

When the doc is published, generate a 3-5 sentence Slack summary the GM can paste into the client channel.

## Format

```
hey [primary contact], dropped a [6-month / engagement-to-date / Q2] review in the [client] notion:
[link to notion page]

quick read on the highlights:
- [strongest YoY or intra-year stat]
- [struggling location progress if relevant]
- [the ask, one line]

[any caveats or context the GM needs to flag: e.g., "still need your sign-off on BOGA"]
```

## Examples

### Full engagement (has YoY + struggling locations)

```
hey kelly, dropped a 6-month review in the goop kitchen notion:
[link]

quick read:
- same-store +60% sales / +58% payout YoY (6 stores ex-BH), driven mostly by the UE cannibalization fix
- SJ + Pas growing on the back of the menu CVR + ratings work, sales starting to follow leading indicators
- big ask: 60-90 more days of runway at SJ + Pas to let the flywheels compound, and a thumbs up on BOGA (parked 11 days)

let me know if anything looks off or you want me to walk through any of it
```

### Newer client (no YoY)

```
hey [name], engagement-to-date review for [client] is live:
[link]

key reads:
- weekly sales $X → $Y over the engagement, ROAS Xx → Yx
- [tier or focus]: progress on [specific metric]
- ask: [specific decision]

happy to walk through it whenever
```

### Tense reflection situation (§5 included)

```
hey [name], wanted to share a 6-month review with you before our next sync:
[link]

honest read: leading with where we owe better on communication, then the data:
- [the structural fix we're shipping this week]
- [the YoY or intra-year headline]
- [the ask]

would love your reaction whenever you've got a few min
```

## Voice rules for the Slack summary

- Lowercase casual (mirrors how Maxx writes on Slack)
- No em-dashes
- Skip greetings beyond the name
- "let me know" / "happy to walk through" rather than formal closings
- Skip "looking forward to your feedback" / "please review at your earliest convenience"
- One ask max: don't pile

## When the summary is for an INTERNAL Slack channel (#int-[client] or #team-spice)

Different format: flag the work, surface anomalies, prep the team for the client's reaction:

```
:memo: exec review for [client] is up .  [link]

internal flags:
- [anything anomalous in the data the team should know about]
- [any reframing that may surprise the client]
- [what we're expecting them to push back on]

reviewing with [client lead] [day/time]
```
