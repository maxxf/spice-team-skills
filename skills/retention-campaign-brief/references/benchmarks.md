# Benchmarks (by campaign type)

Use these to set targets in every brief. If actuals fall short by more than 10%, flag in the next bi-weekly meeting.

## Welcome email

- Open rate: 50%+ (this is a hot list — anyone below 45% has a problem)
- CTR: 15%+
- Conversion within 7 days: 25%+

## Winback (60-day lapsed)

- Open rate: 35-40%
- CTR: 10-12%
- Redemption rate: 5-8%
- Return customer rate within 14 days: 10%+
- Thanx winback average: 6x ROI, 30% reactivation

## Review request (Day 3-5 post-order)

- Open rate: 40-45%
- CTR: 12-15%
- Review conversion: 3-5% (industry standard)
- Target output: 10-15 new reviews per month across platforms

## Loyalty tier upgrade

- Open rate: 55-65% (very high engagement expected)
- CTR: 20-25%
- Order within 7 days: 30%+
- Tier retention: 70%+ maintain or advance

## Birthday

- Open rate: 50-60%
- Redemption: 45-60% (industry: birthday emails have 31% higher AOV)

## Cart abandonment (online orders)

- Recovery rate: 10-15% typical

## Bounceback (1st → 2nd visit)

- 1st to 2nd visit conversion: 25-30% within 14 days

## Standard promo / LTO

- Open rate: 25-30%
- CTR: 4-6%
- Revenue per recipient: $0.30-$0.80 depending on offer strength

## Per-client overlay (real performance)

- **HealthNut** open rates: 66-72%. Anything below 60% means the subject or list segmentation went wrong.
- **MBFS** open rates: 40%+ consistent.
- **Ahipoki** capture rate (% revenue from known members): 16.6% (benchmark is 15%).

## EDR (Effective Discount Rate) target

2.5-3.5% of revenue. Above 4% means the program is over-discounting.

Formula: (Cost of Rewards Redeemed) / (Revenue Generated) × 100.
