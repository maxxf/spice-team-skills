# Review Reply Templates

Universal templates for the $5 review incentive program. One template per star tier. Swap in the brand name and platform, copy, paste, submit.

This program runs 2-3 months per client. These templates are designed to be fast, consistent, and require minimal customization. For clients who want ongoing, personalized review management, that's a separate service.

## Template Variables

- `{brand}` = Restaurant brand name
- `{platform}` = "Uber Eats" or "DoorDash"

---

## 5 Stars

> Thank you for the kind words! We're so glad you enjoyed your {brand} order. As a thank you for leaving a review, we've added a $5 credit to your next {platform} order. Hope to see you again soon!

## 4 Stars

> Thanks for the great feedback! We're always working to make your {brand} experience even better. As a thank you, we've added a $5 credit to your next {platform} order. Hope to earn that 5th star next time!

## 3 Stars

> Thank you for taking the time to share your feedback. We're sorry it wasn't a 5-star experience. We've added a $5 credit to your next {platform} order because we'd love a chance to make it up to you.

## 2 Stars

> We're sorry to hear about your experience. Your feedback helps us get better. We've added a $5 credit to your next {platform} order, and we'd love the chance to turn things around for you.

## 1 Star

> We're really sorry about your experience. Your feedback has been shared with our team. We've added a $5 credit to your next {platform} order. We'd love to make this right.

---

## Edge Cases

**No text, just a star rating:** Use the template for that star level as-is.

**Profanity or abusive language:** Still reply using the 1-star template. Don't engage with the language.

**Escalate before replying if the review mentions:** health/safety issues, legal threats, suspected fraud, or names a specific employee. Tag the GM.
