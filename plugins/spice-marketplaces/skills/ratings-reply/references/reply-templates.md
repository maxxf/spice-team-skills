# Review Reply Templates

Universal templates for the $5 review incentive program. One template per rating tier. Swap in the brand name and platform, copy, paste, submit.

DoorDash uses a three-tier rating system: Loved, Liked, and Didn't like. Uber Eats uses a similar thumbs up/down system. These templates map to both.

This program runs 2-3 months per client. These templates are designed to be fast, consistent, and require minimal customization. For clients who want ongoing, personalized review management, that's a separate service.

## Template Variables

- `{brand}` = Restaurant brand name
- `{platform}` = "Uber Eats" or "DoorDash"

---

## Loved (Positive)

> Thank you for the kind words! We're so glad you enjoyed your {brand} order. As a thank you for leaving a review, we've added a $5 credit to your next {platform} order. Hope to see you again soon!

## Liked (Neutral)

> Thanks for the feedback! We're always working to make your {brand} experience even better. As a thank you, we've added a $5 credit to your next {platform} order. Hope to earn that "Loved" next time!

## Didn't Like (Negative)

> We're sorry to hear about your experience. Your feedback has been shared with our team so we can do better. We've added a $5 credit to your next {platform} order. We'd love the chance to make it right.

---

## Uber Eats Note

Uber Eats auto-reply uses a single universal message for all ratings (no conditional logic). Use this:

> Thank you for taking the time to leave a review! Your feedback helps us improve. As a thank you, we've added a $5 credit to your next Uber Eats order with us. We appreciate your support and hope to serve you again soon!

---

## Edge Cases

**No text, just a rating (e.g., "Liked" with no comment):** Use the template for that tier as-is.

**Profanity or abusive language:** Still reply using the "Didn't like" template. Don't engage with the language.

**Escalate before replying if the review mentions:** health/safety issues, legal threats, suspected fraud, or names a specific employee. Tag the GM.

---

## Character Limit

DoorDash reply field has a **300 character limit**. All templates above fit within this. If you personalize (add customer name, reference a specific dish), watch the count.
