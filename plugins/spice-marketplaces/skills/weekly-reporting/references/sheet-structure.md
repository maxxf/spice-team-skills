# Sheet Structure

## Tab 1: Platform Overview (4 sections x 17 metrics)
Sections: OVERVIEW, UBER EATS, DOORDASH, GRUBHUB
Metrics: Total Net Sales, Marketing Driven Sales, Organic Sales, Total Orders, Orders from Marketing, Organic Orders, AOV, Ad Spend, Offers/Discounts, Total Marketing Spend, Marketing Spend/Sales%, Marketing ROAS, Marketing CPO, Net Payout, Net Payout%, Merchant Fees, Error Charges

## Tab 2: By Location (15 metrics per location)
Same as above minus Merchant Fees/Error Charges, plus Blended ROAS and Blended Menu CVR%

## CSV Format
Platform Overview: Section, Metric, Value
By Location: Location, Metric, Value
