# spice-design

Design briefs and creative production for Spice Digital. Built for Dilli Dias (designer) and anyone briefing creative work.

## Skills (2)

- `ratings-flyer` — submit a 2-sided printed ratings flyer brief that drops in delivery bags and drives review velocity on Uber Eats and DoorDash. Trigger on "ratings flyer for [client]", "$5 flyer", "delivery bag flyer".
- `email-template-designer` — design high-fidelity email marketing templates for restaurant clients, pulling brand context from Notion and outputting Figma-importable HTML. Trigger on "design email for [client]", "email template for [client]".

## Required connections

| Connector | Used by |
|---|---|
| Notion | both (brand context, brief publishing) |
| Slack | ratings-flyer (`#design-requests` post) |
| Figma | email-template-designer (brand library + asset export) |
