# Send time sweet spots (per client + per channel)

These are starting points. Validate against actual campaign performance in the monthly report.

## MBFS

- **Best email day**: Wednesday or Thursday
- **Best email time**: 11am ET (lunch decision window)
- **Worst time**: Friday afternoon, weekend mornings
- **SMS** (if used): 11am-1pm ET, Mon-Thu

## HealthNut

- **Best email day**: Sunday evening or Monday morning (LA wellness routine planning)
- **Best email time**: 7am PT (early risers, fitness crowd) or 5pm PT (post-work)
- **Worst time**: Tuesday-Wednesday lunch (saturated inbox)
- **SMS**: not yet a primary channel

## Ahipoki

- **Best email day**: Thursday or Friday (weekend planning)
- **Best email time**: 10am PT or 4pm PT (lunch / dinner planning)
- **Worst time**: Monday mornings (inbox fatigue)
- **SMS** (once Klaviyo live): 11am-7pm PT, weekdays preferred

## Universal rules

- Never send before 9am client local time
- Never send after 7pm client local time for promotional emails
- Never send Sunday morning before noon
- SMS quiet hours: 9am-9pm local minimum (TCPA is 8am-8pm but Spice tightens)
- Push notifications: any time but coordinate with email so customer doesn't get both within 15 minutes

## Send-time A/B test ideas

If you've never tested send time for a client, propose one of these:

- Tuesday 11am vs Thursday 11am (same client)
- 7am vs 5pm same day
- Weekend morning vs weekend afternoon
- Day-before vs day-of an event/holiday

Run with at least 5K recipients per arm to get a statistically meaningful read.
